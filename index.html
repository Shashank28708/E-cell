About E-Cell

The Tula's Institute Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell) is dedicated to fostering a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship on campus. We empower students to transform ideas into impactful startups through mentorship, workshops, and networking opportunities. Our mission is to support aspiring entrepreneurs at every stage, from ideation to launch, and to build a vibrant startup ecosystem within our college community.
